Policies and Fees

Students will have 30 minues, 45 minutes, or one hour lessons once a week. The duration of the lesson depends on the student's level, and it is subject to change as the student becomes more advanced.

Payment is due at the beginning of each month for the amount of lessons of the month. Cancelations are accepted 24 hours before the scheduled lesson, in which case the canceled lesson is credited for a future lesson. Make-up lessons will be sometimes available.

Call me for fees.
